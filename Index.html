Your Reliever is a well-being companion that helps you manage stress and anxiety through guided content, practical tools, and access to supportive communities and professionals.

We offer breathing and mindfulness exercises, stress and anxiety education, a supportive community forum, and options to connect with licensed professionals.

Stress and Anxiety

Common causes include academic workload, financial pressure, social changes, and limited sleep. Building routines, seeking support, and practicing mindfulness can reduce the impact.

Stress is typically a response to an external pressure and often subsides when the situation resolves. Anxiety can persist even without a clear trigger and may include ongoing worry and physical symptoms.

Depression is distinct. It involves persistent low mood, loss of interest, and functional impairment. Stress and anxiety can co-occur with depression, but diagnosis and care differ. Consider professional support if symptoms persist.
